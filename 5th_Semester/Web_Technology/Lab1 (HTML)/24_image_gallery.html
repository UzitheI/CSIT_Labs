Popular Places in Nepal

Mount Everest

The highest peak in the world, Mount Everest is a majestic Himalayan giant attracting adventurers and mountaineers from around the globe.

Phewa Lake, Pokhara

Surrounded by the Annapurna range, Phewa Lake is a serene freshwater lake in Pokhara, offering stunning views and boat rides.

Boudhanath Stupa

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Boudhanath Stupa is one of the largest stupas in the world and a significant pilgrimage site for Buddhists.
